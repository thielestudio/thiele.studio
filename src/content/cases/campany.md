From a passionate garage-gig to a scalable brand: CAMPANY stands for modular camper van conversions with a design-driven mindset. Initially launched as a custom van conversion service, CAMPANY evolved in response to growing demand, developing a modular furniture system that empowers anyone to build their own camper with ease and flexibility.

This strategic repositioning called for a visual identity that could resonate with a new DIY audience while staying true to the brand's roots. The new name, CAMPANY, unites product, purpose and ambition.

The result is a flexible, distinctive design system that merges craftsmanship, flexibility and freedom into a consistent visual language.

The result: A brand that doesn't just sell furniture, but a promise: Adventure. Your way.
