Qruise is a pioneering German science-tech startup using advanced machine learning to accelerate quantum research. The challenge: to translate highly complex scientific content into a brand that feels both intelligent and approachable, without losing the edge that sets Qruise apart in a fast-evolving field.

Through an intensive 5-day remote Brand Sprint, we defined the strategic foundation and visual DNA of the brand, combining scientific precision with a bold, ownable aesthetic.

The result: a flexible brand design system, event toolkit, print materials and a digital presence that give Qruise the clarity and confidence to lead globally, and inspire the next generation of quantum scientists.

Project done in collaboration with David Latz.
